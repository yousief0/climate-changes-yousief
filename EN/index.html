Climate Changes World Talk
What are climate changes?

Climate change refers to long-term shifts in temperatures and weather patterns. And these transitions could be natural, but since the century Nineteenth, human activities have become the main cause of climate change. This is mainly due to the burning of fossil fuels (such as coal, oil and gas), which produce heat-trapping gases.

What is the impact of climate change on society?

Rising temperatures As concentrations of greenhouse gases rise, the Earth's surface temperature rises.
More severe storms Destructive storms are becoming more intense and more frequent in many areas.
increased dehydration.
Ocean temperature rise.
Food shortage.
More health risks.
poverty and displacement.

What do we do?

What does my country do?

1-Starting with the establishment of the Supreme Council for Climate Change, which was carried out by virtue of the Prime Minister's decision in 2015, and was reconstituted in In 2019, headed by the Prime Minister and the membership of the relevant ministers, to become the body concerned with drawing up general policies to deal with Climate changes and work to develop and update strategies for it.
2- The launch of the National Climate Change Strategy 2050, which aims to address the effects of climate change, is a major part of the country's ability to Planning and managing this file, in order to achieve sustainable economic growth, in addition to adapting to climate changes, as well as improving governance and management work in this file.
3- Launching the plan to modernize the nationally determined contributions, which came out of Egypt's commitment in the Paris Agreement, before the specified date. In November 2022, Egypt will be the first country to carry out this update and present it ahead of schedule.
4- Work on the transition to a green economy, and the state's endeavor to integrate the environmental dimension in all fields.
5- Issuing green bonds that were linked to projects related to facing climate changes, whether in mitigation or adaptation.
6- The Egyptian government sought to green the state's general budget, with the aim of reaching 100% green projects by 2030.

What is COP27

They are annual talks sponsored by the United Nations to discuss the climate change crisis and what the countries of the world are doing to confront this problem. He attends the talks Leaders of 197 countries and thousands of environmental activists, as well as representatives of major industrial companies, especially oil companies, to talk about their participation reduce the levels of pollution they cause.
